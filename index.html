Panner Tikka Rolls Recipe

Servings: 4-6 people

Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 250 gm paneer, cut into small cubes
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 6-8 whole wheat tortillas
  • Chopped cilantro, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the yogurt, ginger paste, garlic paste, cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  2. Add the paneer cubes to the bowl and mix well.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or up to 2 hours.
  4. Preheat a non-stick pan or griddle over medium heat.
  5. Remove the paneer from the refrigerator and add the lemon juice and olive oil. Mix well.
  6. Place a tortilla on the pan and add a few pieces of the marinated paneer in the center.
  7. Fold the tortilla in half to form a half-moon shape and press gently.
  8. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the tortilla is crispy and the paneer is cooked through.
  9. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  10. Serve hot with your favorite chutney or raita.
